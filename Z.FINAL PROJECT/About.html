My Story!

I never went to any culinary School. For me it was not worth the tens of thousand of dollars. I studied science for my higher studies,However I ended up being a chef. I loved cooking. People around me were happy eating my food. I loved experimenting upon various elements of the food, sometimes it used to turn into masterpiece while many times I had to throw the food. I started cooking as a hobby later turned into my passion. I then at my free time applied for a part time job in a famous resturant, it was my aunt's resturant. I got to learn many aspect of cooking. That was the turning point of my life, I started taking cooking seriously. As I said I have experimenting habit, during a experiment the food turned to be the most perfect dish and main served dish in the resturant.

So, One of the my co-worker we formed a strong bond and later we started our way to FoodWay which turned out to be the best decisions of my and my co-worker's life.We decided to give our full focus on FoodWay and then here we have the success. I love how people appreciate our work.That gives me more energy and motivation to make the best food out of the rest.
